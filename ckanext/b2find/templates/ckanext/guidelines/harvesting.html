    Harvesting channels

    EUDAT-B2FIND supports several protocols to fetch metadata records from data provider site

    • Preferably the OAI-PMH v2.0 protocol is used for harvesting metadata directly from the data providers of the research communities. If needed or required, EUDAT gives as well support to set up an OAI-PMH provider.
